The Album

Not a departure.
An arrival.

Comet is the next chapter in an artistic journey that began with cinematic orchestral worlds, moved through emotional pop and rock territories, and now arrives at its most honest destination.

This is not a rock record made for the sake of heaviness. It is a testimony — seven songs drawn from real emotional excavation. Internal chaos. The cost of integrity. The quiet devastation of loss. And the stubborn, unshakeable truth that survives all of it.

The comet does not ask permission to move. It burns because that is its nature. It passes through everything and remains itself.

First Single · 18 Jun 2026

What Broke Me

A Rock Statement

I didn't stay broken.
I became the reason I survived.

The opening statement of the Comet era. A hard rock anthem about survival, reconstruction, and what it means to carry your scars not as damage — but as architecture.

Rock Statement Structure

The rock carries the statement.
The blazing tail carries the emotion.

This is the Comet concept in its clearest form: the core is the message, dense and direct; the tail is the arrangement, expanding that message into movement, force and memory.

I · The Core

Impact

The vocal and lyrical statement behave like the comet rock: compact, heavy and unavoidable. This is where the truth of the song is concentrated.

→

II · The Trail

Afterglow

The guitars, drums, textures and space behave like the blazing tail: they stretch the message outward until it becomes atmosphere, momentum and memory.

Behind the Music

The person
behind the sound.

Not a producer hiding behind a desk. A composer who builds entire emotional worlds — from silence, from memory, from things that couldn't be said any other way.

Based in Dubai, writing music that crosses every border — because emotion has no geography.

Every release is a chapter. Not a single. The titles are deliberate. The sequencing is intentional. Nothing is filler.

100% independent. No label. No committee. The artistic vision is entirely intact — and entirely his.

Next: Comet. A full rock album arriving 21 August 2026 — a deliberate, necessary left turn that the cinematic foundation made possible.
