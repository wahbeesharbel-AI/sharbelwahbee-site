The Structure

Two halves of one truth.

A Rock Statement is built in two movements. The first is spoken. The second is felt. Neither is complete without the other.

The Statement
The words are the stone.

The vocals carry a moment, a realization, a lesson learned — a truth that needs to be spoken. Compact, heavy, unavoidable. This is where the meaning is concentrated.

The Artist

Sharbel Wahbee.

An independent cinematic composer based in Dubai, building entire emotional worlds from silence, memory, and things that couldn't be said any other way.

His work moves through cinematic storytelling, emotional journeys, and human connection — always returning to the same core: resilience and transformation. Every release is a chapter. Nothing is filler.

COMET is the rock arrival the cinematic foundation always pointed toward.
