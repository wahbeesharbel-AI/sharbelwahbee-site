Biography

The story.

Sharbel Wahbee is an independent cinematic composer and songwriter based in Dubai, creating music at the intersection of emotion, memory, and human connection.

After his debut EP Whispers from Beyond — a cinematic and atmospheric exploration of inner worlds — Sharbel expanded his sound into more narrative-driven releases, where melody, voice, and silence serve the story itself.

Through projects like Orbital, his 2026 album, Sharbel invites listeners into a cinematic journey where music is not only heard, but felt — a space where longing, intimacy, and imagination orbit the same emotional core.

His upcoming album Comet, releasing 21 August 2026, represents a deliberate evolution into rock — raw, honest, and built from real emotional excavation. It is not a departure. It is an arrival.

Based in Dubai, UAE — writing music that crosses every border, because emotion has no geography.

Genre range: Cinematic Orchestral, Cinematic Pop, Pop Rock, Alternative Rock, and Rock.
